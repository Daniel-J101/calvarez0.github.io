Research

Artificial Curiosity

Intrinsically motivated goal exploration algorithms for autotelic agents. We are creating AI agents, giving them an environment, and letting them create their own goals. The agent learns about the environment through it's own curiosity. Then we analyze it's internal structures to find out how "conscious" it is (consciousness/understanding is very hard to quantify, we are looking for meaningful representations).

Fine Tuning LLMs to Emulate Instructor

TIC-TOC: Enhancing Industrial Control Education through an Intelligent Tutoring System for Real-Time Interaction and Engagement. Researching the best intelligent tutoring systems and combining them with fine-tuned multi-modal LLMs to create a novel learning experience that emulates individual professor's feedbacks for the engineering department at TAMU.
